Rules and Tournament Info

Open the sections below for the most important tournament rules and practical information.

01 Game format

Games are played according to the official tournament schedule. Pool games determine the placement for playoff games, semifinals and finals.

  • Check the schedule before every round.
  • Be ready at the field before the listed start time.
  • The tournament organizer can adjust timing if needed.
02 Score reporting

After each match, both teams should submit the score and Spirit of the Game points through the Submit Score page.

  • Each team reports once per game.
  • If both teams report the same score, the result is confirmed.
  • If the score does not match, the game is marked as a mismatch for the organizer.
03 Spirit of the Game

Each team gives Spirit points to the opponent after the game. Spirit is scored in five categories from 0 to 4 points each.

  • Rules knowledge
  • Fouls and body contact
  • Fair-mindedness
  • Positive attitude
  • Communication
04 Live, upcoming and finished games

The schedule status updates automatically based on time and submitted results.

  • Upcoming appears shortly before game start.
  • Live appears while the game is expected to be in progress.
  • Finished appears when the time has passed or the score is submitted.
  • Not held means the game is later in the schedule.
05 Practical field rules

Please help keep the tournament running smoothly.

  • Respect field times and move quickly between rounds.
  • Keep sidelines clear and safe.
  • Bring water and be ready for changing weather.
  • Contact the tournament organizer if there is a conflict or score issue.
